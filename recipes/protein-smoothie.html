Protein Smoothie

Description

This high protein smoothie is ideal for a pre-workout meal

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop whey protein
  • 1 banana
  • 4 strawberries
  • 10 blueberries
  • 5 raspberries
  • 4 blackberries
  • 2 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 2 tablespoon oatmeal
  • 2 tablespoon flax seeds
  • 2 tablespoon peanut butter
  • ½ cup kale
  • ½ cup spinach
  • 1 ½ cups of almond milk

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all fruits
  2. Then, add spinach and kale follow by peanut butter
  3. Add to the blender all dry ingredients
  4. Lastly, add milk and blend
  5. Pour in glass and enjoy!
